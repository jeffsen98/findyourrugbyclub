About This Directory

Everything you need to know about the USA Club Rugby finder.

What is this?

The USA Club Rugby Directory is an interactive map and searchable database of club rugby teams across the United States. Whether you're new to the sport, relocating, or looking to find a team near you, this tool helps you discover clubs at every level — from D1 Premier down to Developmental.

How does it work?

Club data is maintained in a Google Sheet and published live to this site. Each club entry includes their name, location, division, home field, practice schedule, and contact information. The map automatically updates as new clubs are added or existing entries are edited.

Divisions

D1 — Premier

The top tier of USA club rugby. D1 clubs compete through regional conferences and playoffs with a national championship on the line.

D2 — Championship

Highly competitive clubs that battle through regional play to earn a spot at the D2 National Championship.

D3 — Community

Community-rooted clubs that compete regionally and nationally, welcoming players of all backgrounds and experience levels.

Want to add or update your club?

If your club is missing or you need to update your information, reach out and we'll get it added to the directory.
