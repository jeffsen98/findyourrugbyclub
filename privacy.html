Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

The USA Club Rugby Directory is an unofficial, fan-made community project. We are committed to being transparent about what information we collect and how we use it. In short: we collect very little, and we don't sell or share anything.

Information We Collect

This site does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from visitors. Club data displayed on the map is sourced from publicly available information and submitted by club representatives.

Contact Form

If you submit a message through our Contact page, we collect only the information you provide (your name, email, and message). This is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not stored beyond that purpose.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sheets - for storing and serving club data
  • Leaflet.js - for map rendering
  • CartoDB - for map tile imagery
  • Google Fonts - for typography

Cookies

This site does not use cookies or any local tracking technologies.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

This is an unofficial, fan-made community directory. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by USA Rugby.
